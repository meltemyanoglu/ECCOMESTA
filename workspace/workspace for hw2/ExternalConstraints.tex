
\subsection{External Constraints}
\begin{itemize}
  \item A product can belong only one category and can be supplied by only one supplier at a time.
  \item An employee can work at only one store and also can hold only one position at a time.
  \item A receipt can be generated by only one employee and at only one store at a time, and also receipt can be associated with only one customer and one product.
  
\end{itemize}
