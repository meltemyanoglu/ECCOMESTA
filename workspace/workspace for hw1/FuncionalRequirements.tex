\section{Functional Requirements}

The database system must store:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Stakeholders}, including: Data analysts, HR Personnel, IT specialists,
    \item \textbf{Employees}, including: Stock clerks, Store manager, Inventory control specialist, Cashier
    \item \textbf{Record the details related to each task}; as for example adding/deleting a new item or update an item's quantity at a specific shop, data validation and verification to ensure data accuracy
    \item \textbf{Stakeholders data}, including: name and surname, job description, 
    \item \textbf{Stores data}, including: store code, name, address, location, stock level, employee list; 
    \item \textbf{Products data}, including: product code, name, price, VAT, category, supplier code;
    \item \textbf{Users data}, including: name, surname, their addresses, e-mail addresses and preferred store
    \item \textbf{Receipts data}, including: List of products, date, Payment method, store location; 
\end{itemize}
The system must allow; 
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Employees and stakeholders} to login and reach their personalized page; 
    \item \textbf{Clients} to login and sign up; 
    \item \textbf{Clients} to change/update their information;
    \item \textbf{HR personnel} to add/delete a new manager/employee to a store;
    \item \textbf{HR personnel} to promote/demote an employee; 
    \item \textbf{Stock clerks} to add/delete a new product in their store;
    \item \textbf{Stock clerks} to update a quantity of a specific product in their store;
    \item \textbf{Managers} to update the database of products, add/delete a product in deemed necessary; 
    \item \textbf{Data analysts} to check stores and sales/orders; 
    \item \textbf{Inventory control specialist} to order new products to the store;
    \item \textbf{Manage multiple arrangements} by each team/shop;
    \item \textbf{Data analysts} to conduct statistical exploration from sales/orders of products; 
    \item \textbf{IT specialists} to maintain the database and interfere if necessary;
    \item \textbf{Transfer of goods} or employees from one store to another;
    

\end{itemize}
