\section{Functional Requirements}

The database must store all the data and metadata relative to acquisition sessions and all the connected information to correctly interpret them in the anomaly detection system. In particular, the system must store:
\\
\\
• Stakehlders, including: Data analysts, HR Personnel, IT specialists, \\
• Employees, including Stock clerks, Store manager, Inventory control specialist, \\
• Record the details related to each task; as for example adding/deleting a new item or update an item's quantity at a specific shop, \\
• Stakeholders data, including: name and surname, job description, \\
• Stores' name, address, location and e-mail; \\
• Employees of the specific shop center, including the manager; \\
• Categories and products with quantities belong to the specific store; \\

The system must allow; \\
• Employees and stakeholders to login and reach their personalized page; \\
• Clients to login and sign up; \\
• Clients to change their information; \\
• Managers to add/delete a new employee to their store; \\
• Stakeholders to add/delete a new manager to a store; \\
• Employees to add/delete a new product in their store; \\
• Employees to update a quantity of a specific product in their store; \\
• Stakeholders/Managers to check stores and sales/orders; \\
• Stakeholders/Managers to order new products to a store; \\
