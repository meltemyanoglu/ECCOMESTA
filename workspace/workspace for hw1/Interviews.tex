\section{Interviews}
%who are we going to interview in order to obtain the proper information on the system?
%do we interview the final user? the managers? the analysts?


Several interviews were conducted to gather system requirements.

\vspace{5mm} %5mm vertical space

Managers: administerial stakeholder interviews aid in understanding the company's strategic goals and requirements, guiding the structuring of the database system to meet articulated demands.

\vspace{5mm} %5mm vertical space

IT technicians: to clarify the current technical infrastructure to enable the integration of the planned database system, define the constraints of the prevailing systems, and articulate the prerequisites for compatibility.

\vspace{5mm} %5mm vertical space

Final users: in order to be informed about final users' expectations from the system in order to provide efficient service and support to customers. In addition, information about the daily tasks that need to be completed and the difficulties encountered during this process is provided.
